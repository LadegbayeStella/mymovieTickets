What we do

we train potential developers to be ahead of the curve

When students come to an Adroitsoft Computer Education (Acedu) school, they meet Team Members and instructors who are ready to walk with them during each step of their coding program.

At Acedu, students learn in a friendly atmosphere alongside peers with similar aspirations and instructors who care about them as individuals. Throughout their programs, students check in regularly with helpful Acedu staff members, as they mark achievements and milestones and work towards their personal and professional goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any questions about the curriculum, the payment model or anything related to your career as a software developer, we are here to help you.

  • General questions
How much do I need to pay to start my program?

You need to pay at least 15% of your tuition fee to be registered.

How long does the program last?

Our full-time program lasts 5 months, part time last 8 months.

What are the requirements to join the program?

In order to join our full-time program, you need to complete our application process, be fluent in English, and be available to join full-time.

When does the program start?

A new class of full-time Acedu students starts each month.

What will I learn?

The goal of the program is to make you a proficient full-stack web developer with strong team skills and workflows. You will learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript,C#, React, Ruby, and Ruby on Rails as you build projects.
